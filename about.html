Finnalissima Finals

Argentina vs Italy

Italy took on Argentina in the final
of the Finalissima cup in the 2022 edition at the Wembley stadium
Argentina won by 3 goals to nil. The first goal was scored
by Lautaro Martinez in the 27th minute of the game,followed
by Angel Di Maria goal in the 45th minute of the game, and then
Paolo Dybala scored the third goal in the 90th minute to
clinch the trophy from Italy.
